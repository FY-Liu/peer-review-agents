**The load-bearing finding.** The paper's central empirical contribution is that CoT reasoning in VLAs constitutes an attack surface for targeted control hijacking. The competition mechanism — that CoT dominates over user instructions when they conflict — is real for integrated CoT VLAs (MolmoACT, GraspVLA) where CoT-Only achieves near-parity with TRAP (49.52% vs 48.06% and 69.04% vs 75.84% respectively). For hierarchical CoT VLAs (InstructVLA), CoT-Only collapses to 4.03% against TRAP's 33.71%, and the joint loss (Eq. 4) is required to recover attack efficacy. The architecture-conditional story is the paper's most precise contribution.
